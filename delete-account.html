How to request deletion of your DIP account and data

DIP allows you to delete your account and all associated personal data at any time, either from within the app or by contacting us directly.

Option 1: Delete from within the app

The fastest way to delete your account is directly from the DIP app:

  1. Open the DIP app and go to your Profile
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear icon)
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Type DELETE to confirm — this prevents accidental deletion
  6. Tap "Delete My Account" to confirm

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

Email: privacy@dipdating.com

Subject line: Account Deletion Request

Please include the email address associated with your DIP account so we can locate and process your request. We will respond within 30 days and may require you to verify your identity before processing the deletion.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed within 30 days:

  • Profile information: name, bio, job title, education, height, interests, preferences, and profile prompts
  • Profile photos: all photos you uploaded to your profile
  • Account credentials: your email address, authentication tokens, and login history
  • Location data: your stored coordinates, city, and geohash
  • Matches: all match records associated with your profile
  • Dips: Dips you created or were featured in
  • Subscription data: your subscription status and tier information
  • Notification preferences: all stored notification and privacy settings

What data may be retained

In accordance with our Privacy Policy, certain data may be retained after account deletion for legal, safety, and operational purposes:

  • Messages and conversations: retained for legal and moderation purposes
  • Moderation records: reports, warnings, suspensions, bans, and appeal records are retained indefinitely for safety and legal purposes
  • Verification materials: selfie photos and ID documents may be retained for a reasonable period after deletion for fraud prevention
  • Anonymised analytics: aggregated, anonymised data may be retained indefinitely

Important: Cancel your subscription separately

If you have an active DIP Gold subscription, deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. To avoid continued billing, you must cancel your subscription separately:

  • Google Play: Open Google Play Store → Profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → DIP → Cancel
  • Apple App Store: Open Settings → Your name → Subscriptions → DIP → Cancel
