5. Matchmaking System (Dips, Matches, and Date Updates)

5.1 Dips

A "Dip" is a user-generated matchmaking suggestion created by a Matcher who believes two users would be compatible. Dips include a written explanation of why the Matcher believes the pairing would work. Dips are visible in the community feed and may be endorsed (hearted), commented on, and discussed by other users.

Free-tier users may create up to 5 Dips per day. Gold subscribers may create unlimited Dips. We reserve the right to adjust these limits at any time.

5.2 Match Threshold and Reveal

When a sufficient number of Matchers independently suggest the same pairing, the match is "revealed" to both users. The required number of Matcher endorsements before a reveal is configurable and may vary. The reveal threshold is an engagement mechanic and does not constitute any form of compatibility assessment, vetting, or endorsement by DIP.

5.3 Date Updates

Matched users may voluntarily share public "Date Updates" — stories about their interactions after a match reveal. Date Updates are visible to the DIP community and require mutual approval from both matched users before publication. Date Updates may include text, photos, and location information that you choose to share. You are solely responsible for the content of any Date Update you post.

5.4 Leaderboard

DIP maintains a leaderboard that tracks Matcher and user statistics including scores, Dips created, success rates, and connections. Leaderboard participation is automatic for all users. Leaderboard data is visible to other users and is filtered by scope (global, local, or custom distance radius).
