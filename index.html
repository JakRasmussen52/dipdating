How it works

Three steps to a real match

No algorithm. No swiping in the dark. Just people who spotted something between two people and decided to do something about it.

1

A Matcher spots something

Anyone on DIP — a friend, a mutual, or a complete stranger — sees your profile and thinks they know someone perfect for you. They create a Dip with a note explaining why.

2

Others agree and Dip too

Other Matchers see the same pair and add their own endorsement. When enough people think you belong together, the match threshold is reached.

3

The match is revealed

Both of you can see who the Matchers were, the notes they wrote, and why multiple people think you'd work. Then you decide whether to say hello.
